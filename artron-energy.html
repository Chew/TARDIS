Artron Energy

Your TARDIS runs on Artron Energy, and has multiple ways of recharging. By default the wood button on the TARDIS console is how you charge and check your energy levels. Traveling and hiding your TARDIS use up energy.

Getting started

The first thing you will want to do is jump start your TARDIS with energy.

To jumpstart your tardis to 50% energy, please right click the wood button with your “TARDIS key” in hand.

Full charge

To fully charge your TARDIS, right click the wood button with a Nether Star to bring it to 100% (This item is configurable in the config file).

Time lord energy

You as a time traveler also build energy over time as you travel. To transfer it to the TARDIS, right click the wood button while sneaking (Shift by default)

The TARDIS energy condenser

You may also convert certain blocks to energy. This requires a chest to be placed inside and for the player to run the /tardis update condenser command, and follow the instructions.

Items that can be condensed are:

Cobble
Dirt
Sand
Gravel
Rotten Flesh

Charged creepers

Charged creepers may be killed for Artron Energy.

Server recharge points

Finally, the last way to charge your TARDIS is to use a server admin created recharging station. These can be created by using a beacon and the /tardisadmin recharger {name} command.

It is also recommended that server admins create a ‘TARDIS area’ for Police Boxes to “park” in, so they can recharge easily.
