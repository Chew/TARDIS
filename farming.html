Mob farming

You can capture farm animals in the Police Box and transport them into the TARDIS.

The animals you can collect are:

  • Chicken
  • Cow
  • Pig
  • Sheep

You will first need to open the Police Box door by clicking it with the TARDIS key while sneaking (usually the left SHIFT key). Once the door is open, herd some animals in there, then close the door.

Enter the TARDIS as usual, and one of two things will happen:

  1. If you have already grown a ‘farm’, the mobs will be teleported into the apprpriate pen in the farm room
  2. If no farm room is available, then the spawn eggs for the mobs are placed in the player’s inventory. Note: You will need to remove mob_spawning: deny from the TARDIS world’s region file if you are using WorldGuard.
