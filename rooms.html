Rooms

Note: This Feature Requires create_worlds: true in the config, and a multi-world plugin.

Growing rooms

You can create rooms in your TARDIS. Each room costs a varying amount of Artron Energy. To create a room, simply place the required material (the seed) where you would like the door to be.

Use the command:

/tardis room [room type]

and follow the instructions. The room types and their corresponding seed blocks are as follows: 

Empty - GLASS
Bedroom - GLOWSTONE
Kitchen - PUMPKIN
Vault - DISPENSER
Arboretum - LEAVES
Library - BOOKCASE
Pool - SNOW block
Passage - CLAY block
Gravity well - MOSSY COBBLESTONE

Note: The gravity well room type is gravity and requires the seed block to be placed in the middle of an empty room.

Once the room is finished generating, a door will appear at the chosen location and a confirmation message will be shown.

Removing rooms

Rooms may also be removed (or in TARDIS terms - jettisoned).

Simply place TNT on the interior entrance of the room and use the command:

/tardis jettison [room type]

Then follow the onscreen instructions.
