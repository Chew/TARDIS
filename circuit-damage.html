Circuit damage

If configured, circuits can become damaged after use. When the tardis malfunctions there is a 50% chance that a circuit will become damaged even more than usual.

Circuits can be repaired by combining them with other damaged circuits or redstone in an anvil.

circuits:
    damage: false
    uses:
        scanner: 20
        memory: 20
        materialisation: 50
        ARS: 20
        chameleon: 18
        randomiser: 50
        temporal: 20
        input: 50
        invisibility: 5
    level: easy
